Chicken Biryani

Description

This recipe is from india and will be very flavorful with lots of spices. If you follow these steps exactly you will make the most authentic chicken biryani.Before going into the process lets look at the ingredients list below.

ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Basmati rice
  • Whole spices
  • Coriander leaves
  • Mint leaves
  • green chillies
  • Brown Onions
  • Yougurt
  • Ghee
  • Biryani masala
  • Coriander powder
  • Cooking oil

Cooking method:

  1. First, let's wash the chicken throughly and marinate with all the ingredients like whole spices,green chillies,mint, coriander leaves, chilli powder,salt, brown onions,oil, biryani masala, coriander powder and Yougurt. Let this sit for 2 hours or overnight in fridge.
  2. Next let's start cooking the chicken. In a biryani pot, add the marinated chicken and cover and cook for 30 minutes. Be sure to mix gently in between so it won't stick to the bottom.
  3. Paralley, when the chciken is cooking lets cook the rice too. Take a pot and add water once the water starts to boil add the soaked rice.You need to be very careful when cooking rice, as it should not be overcooked. let the rice cook 70%, then strain it and keep it aside.
  4. After the chicken is cooked add the rice on top also add some mint, coriander leaves and some ghee. Now, close the lid and cook it on dum in a very low flame for 30 minutes.Once done remove from the fire and open after 30 minutes. Now, your delicious chicken dum biryani is ready to eat!
